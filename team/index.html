Team

Our team varies from project to project and the governance is mainly DAC (decentralized autonomous community) oriented on a per project basis. We run our projects with a SCRUM mindset, giving the complete autonomy to each team member to self-manage, as is the rule in most open source communities.

In order to facilitate the communication with clients all projects have a single product owner (PO), and are run under the technical supervision of Pedro Reis Cola√ßo (prc), our lead technology researcher and developer, due to his experience in the blockchain, peer-to-peer and decentralized systems and broader experience in software engineering.

We defend that engineers in this space have a very important role in defining the next generation of our global economic system, with all its impact in peoples lives, so our involvement in any project also depends on its alignment with each team members perspective and values.

We work everyday to make the world a better place for all humanity! This will always be our vision and main focus, despite any economic or political incentive.
